CPU-Friendly Continuous Learning

A novel approach to building LLM-like capabilities using bitsets, compression-based learning, and permanent RL - all running efficiently on CPU without large matrix operations.

Essence & Grouping

Groups become stable "identity of meaning" through predictive co-occurrence.

Direct & Indirect Deduction

Multi-hop reasoning through temporal transitions and graph traversal.

Continuous RL

Every conversation becomes a learning opportunity with importance-weighted updates.

Interpretable

Groups are explicit lists of identities - no opaque embeddings.
